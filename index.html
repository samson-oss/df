About us

African Sun is Zimbabwe’s leading hotel asset management company with a portfolio of hotels that is unparalleled in variety, location, and size

Our fine mix of select hotels including internationally recognized brands some of which have achieved world status amongst the "Top 100 Hotels in the World" makes leisure, business, conferencing and incentives our specialty. The Group has hotels in every major tourist resort and in four out of the five major cities in the country, bringing that long-awaited tour of Zimbabwe on your doorstep. Whether you are travelling as part of a group, with someone special, or as a single traveler, unforgettable experiences await you under the African Sun.
